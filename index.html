Market Opportunity

Independent

Volume Game

38,000 dealers in USA  ·  Competitors: DealerCenter, CarsForSale, Dealer Car Search

  • ✕ Highly price sensitive — low switching cost
  • ✕ Intense competition, limited differentiation
  • ✕ Low margin, high churn risk
  • ✕ Requires high volume to scale
  • ✓ Large total addressable market (38K)
  • ✓ Current cash-flow base (263 active dealers)
Franchise

Enterprise Game

15,842 dealers in USA  ·  Competitors: Dealer Inspire, DealerOn, NetDriven

  • ✓ Higher ACV & larger, longer-term contracts
  • ✓ OEM-driven standardization
  • ✓ Access to CRM installed base
  • ✓ End-to-end retail journey capability
  • ✓ Stronger retention via certifications
  • ✓ Enterprise positioning opportunity
02

Current Challenges

CH-01
OEM Certification Loss

Loss of OEM certifications limits franchise market access and enterprise credibility. Only one active certification remains (Porsche).

CH-02
Weak Product-Market Fit

Weak PMF in the independent segment driven by low pricing pressure and commoditization — making differentiation and margin growth difficult.

CH-03
Website Instability

Core infrastructure instability undermines sales credibility, customer trust, and the ability to pitch the product as enterprise-grade.

CH-04
Limited Sales Resources

5-person independent sales team. Zero dedicated franchise sales staff — a critical gap for pursuing enterprise pipeline.

CH-05
DNS Management Risk

Risk of customer attrition due to failure to retain DNS management controls. Loss of DNS = loss of account leverage.

CH-06
Feature Parity Gap

Missing key franchise-critical capabilities vs. DealerOn and Dealer Inspire: digital retailing (Buy Online), AI chat, behavioral personalization, save & compare.

Certification & Entry Plan  6–12 Months

Phase 1
Rebuild OEM Certifications

Restore and pursue new OEM certification pathways to unlock franchise market eligibility and enterprise credibility.

Phase 2
Engage Aggregators

Partner with Shift Digital and other aggregators to access distribution. Net 70% revenue retained after 30% commission.

Phase 3
Build Feature Parity

Close critical gaps: digital retailing (Buy Online), AI chat, behavioral personalization, save & compare tools.

Phase 4
Stabilize Infrastructure

Resolve website instability and secure DNS management to reduce churn risk and support an enterprise-grade product pitch.

Phase 5
Hire Franchise Sales Head

Recruit dedicated franchise-focused sales leadership to drive enterprise pipeline and OEM certification relationships.

Recommended Direction

Shift primary focus to the Franchise market.

  • Shift primary commercial focus to the franchise market
  • Maintain independent base as ongoing cash-flow engine
  • Build certification roadmap immediately — 6–12 month horizon
  • Hire dedicated franchise sales leadership
  • Reposition product as an enterprise retail platform solution
  • Independent = Volume Game, Low Margin → Franchise = Enterprise Game, High Margin
